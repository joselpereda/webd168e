Who is Jose Pereda?

Jose Pereda was born in a rural town in Jalisco, Mexico, in a region commonly known as tequila country. He as in parents immigrated to the United States in 1979. He and his six siblings were raised in the suburbs of Los Angeles. As of today, he is the only one of those siblings that decided to leave the nest and move to a different city. Married nearly 20 years. His wife and he have five children, in every age range. The eldest is 24 and the youngest just over two years of age. Up until a few years back Mr. Pereda managed business operations for a global conglomerate. Fist in the United States and later in Latin America. This fact provided the opportunity for him to visit a variety of country. And yes, tons of airports and hotels.

Let's dive a little deeper...

Professional Career

During most of his professional career Mr. Pereda worked in the technology industry. Software development to be move precise. His skills range from hardware design, business development, product management, and technical sales. Among his accomplishments was introducing a new line of business with the main telecommunications companies in Latin America and the Caribbean. Within his role as business development manager, he was privileged with identifying new innovated products and services, shepherding them through incubation and bringing some of them to market.

Family

Behind Mr. Pereda is a beautiful family consisting of his wife and five children: two daughters and three boys. In all age groups, as mentioned above. His family mantra is "work for what you want...don't expect anyone to give you anything." Said mantra has created a family of go-getters. At times that has meant that mom and dad have also been stretched with their time and resources to enable the pursuit of their goals. Meet his two girls.
